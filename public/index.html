About the beta programme

Two telescopes.
Pointed at the same sky.

We built CDP on a simple premise: ancient traditions and modern neuroscience are not competing explanations. They observe the same human experience through different instruments. Every reading at every tier can be delivered in two voices — traditional symbolic language, or the same insight translated into salience networks, circadian rhythms, and neuroplasticity.

We are inviting a small, carefully selected group to use the platform before public launch — to stress-test the readings, shape the experience, and tell us honestly what works and what doesn't.
