HOW TO USE CDP
YOUR DAILY FLOW

Morning: open the Reading tab and begin your day with a Full Reading. Tap any card (Day, Moon, Kin, Life Path) for deeper Oracle insight. Scroll through priorities, shadow work, and the week ahead.

Midday: the check-in bar slides up from the bottom. Tap "Reading" to re-anchor. Evening: scroll to the reflection section to close the day intentionally.

THE TABS
Reading — your full daily Oracle across all four frameworks
Daily Card — a single shareable card with today's synthesis
Calendar — monthly view showing Kin, moon phases, GAP portals and master number days. Tap any day to preview its energy and open a reading for it.
My Energy — your personal numerology, Life Path, Personal Year, and Chinese astrology profile
Compatibility — enter a second person's birth data for a relationship reading across all frameworks
Profile — save your birth details, time, location, and personal context
History — your past readings, tap any to reopen
READING DEPTHS
✦ Free — Universal Day, moon phase, Kin. Instant.
⚡ Full Reading — all four frameworks, priorities, shadow work, week ahead. ~15 seconds.
☽ Monthly Arc — full reading plus lunation map and wavespell arc. 2-4 minutes.
◉ Yearly Reading — natal chart integration, yearly arc, transit themes. 3-5 minutes.
◈ Oracle Deep Dive — maximum depth, all frameworks, Hellenistic lots. 5-10 minutes.
SPECIAL DAYS
★ GAP Portal — Galactic Activation Portal. The veil is thinner. Watch for synchronicities.
⚠ Black Moon — two days before the new moon. Do not initiate. Complete and release.
✨ Shiva Moon — two days after the new moon. Plant intentions. Generative energy.
11, 22, 33, 44 — master number days. Heightened frequency. Tap the day number for its meaning.
READING LANGUAGE

On the entry screen, choose Ancient for the traditional cosmic language of archetypes and energetic fields, or Modern for the same insights translated into neuroscience: salience networks, circadian rhythms, interoception and neuroplasticity.

PROFILES

Use the Profiles button (☿ in the top bar) to save multiple people — family, friends, colleagues. Select a profile before a reading to receive it for that person. Use Compatibility to read two profiles together.

GETTING MORE FROM THE ORACLE

Tap any section heading or card to ask the Oracle a follow-up question. Pre-built chips appear for common questions, or type your own.

The more personal context you add in your profile (what you are building, who matters to you, what you are navigating), the more specific the Oracle becomes. It speaks to your actual life, not a generic one.
